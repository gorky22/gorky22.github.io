3D position reconstruction of reentry objects fragments using two video recordings.
Author: Bc. Damián Gorčák
Supervisor: Mgr. Daniel Kyselica
Consultant: Mgr. Jiří Šilha, PhD.
ABOUT THE PROJECT

Our project aims to reconstruct the 3D position of reentry object fragments by analyzing two video recordings, while utilizing Astropy to convert the captured altitude and azimuth coordinates to geocentric coordinates for accurate point location. The growing number of untracked object reentries poses a significant challenge, and our approach will help in predicting and mitigating potential impact points on the ground.
